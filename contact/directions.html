Directions From Cape Town International Airport

  • Head south on Drop-and-Go toward Airport Ring Road (500 m). 
  • Merge onto Airport Ring Road (500 m).
  • At the roundabout, take the first exit onto Airport Approach Road (2.2 km)
  • Follow N2 to Philip Kgosana Drive / M3. 
  • Take the M3 / De Waal Drive exit from N2 - +/- 8 min (11.6 km)
  • Merge onto N2 (11.3 km)
  • Use the left 2 lanes to take the M3 / De Waal Drive exit toward Cape Town S/Table Mountain (300 m)
  • Follow M3 to Kloof Street in Gardens +/- 8 min (5.2 km)
  • Continue onto Philip Kgosana Dr/M3 (3.1 km)
  • Slight left onto Jutland Avenue / M3 and continue to follow M3 (1.9 km)
  • Turn left onto Kloof Street. Kloof Street Hotel is located on the corner of Kloof Street & Rheede Street
  • The secure basement parking entrance is on Dorman Street, a one way street that can be accessed off Kloof Street
  • The hotel entrance is at 8 Rheede Street

Parking Costs: 

Secure basement paid parking is available in our garage, and comes with the added peace of mind of having security onsite. Please note that parking is limited and is an additional cost of R140 per vehicle per day. The entrance to the parking garage is halfway down Dorman Street on your right hand side. Dorman Street is a one way street off Kloof Street. This cost can be settled upon arrival at our front desk.  

T's & C's apply. Parking is limited and subject to availability. Costs are subject to change.
