FAQ’s - Kloof Street Hotel

Find answers to frequently asked questions about Kloof Street Hotel in Cape Town, including parking, amenities, nearby attractions, and more.

Is there a heated pool at Kloof Street Hotel?

Our pool is not heated but it is clean and refreshing year-round.

What time does Kloof Street Hotel’s restaurant close?

The Sanctuary Restaurant closes at 10:00 PM daily.

Is there secure parking at Kloof Street Hotel?

Yes, secure undercover parking is available in our basement, accessible from Dorman Street, at an additional cost per vehicle per night. This can be settled upon arrival at reception, or added as an extra when you book online.

Does Kloof Street Hotel offer a 24-hour check-in service?

Yes, we do offer 24-hour check-in service for your convenience.

Are guests allowed to use general hotel areas before check-in & after check-out?

Absolutely! Guests are welcome to use our communal areas before check-in and after check-out before departing. We have a large lounge area that is perfect for killing some time before your room may be ready for check in from 2pm.

Can I book a late check-out at Kloof Street Hotel?

We offer a late check-out service subject to availability and an additional fee. Please email reservations@lionroars.com prior to checking in as this service is only subject to room availability.

Does Kloof Street Hotel have an iron/ironing board in the rooms?

While irons and ironing boards are not provided in rooms, laundry services are available for a nominal fee. Please advise management for collection. Laundry bags are provided in your suite cupboard along with a list of charges. The laundry charge will be added to your general bill and may be settled on departure. Dry cleaning is also available from an outsourced company. Please inquire at reception for more information.

Does Kloof Street Hotel have a fridge/minibar in their rooms?

Yes, all of our rooms are equipped with a minibar. Please note that minibar items are chargeable. Should you have any special stock requests for your minibar just pop us an email on reservations@lionroars.com before your stay.

Is it easy to get a taxi or uber from Kloof Street Hotel?

Taxis and rideshare apps such as Uber and Bolt are available from our central location in town. Our friendly staff can also assist you with arranging transportation.

What activities or tourist attractions are close to Kloof Street Hotel?

Explore nearby attractions within walking distance or a short drive from our hotel.
- V&A Waterfront (CPT): 3km | 12 minutes drive by car
- Bo-Kaap (CPT): 1.5km | 6 minutes drive by car
- Table Mountain Cable Car (CPT): 4km | 9 minutes drive by car
- Sea Point Promenade (CPT): 5.2km | 15 minutes drive by car
- Franschhoek Wine Tram: 75.9km | 1-hour drive by car

Does Kloof Street Hotel have a gym?

While we don't have a on-site gym in the hotel, Cape Town offers various fitness options that you can explore during your stay in the city. From cross fit to yoga to pilates to great running and hiking routes - it just depends what you enjoy. Our team will be happy to help you plan your exercise routine during your stay.

Does Kloof Street Hotel offer shuttle services?

Yes, we provide shuttle services to and from the airport for our guests' convenience. Please contact reservations@lionroars.com for details and bookings.

Does Kloof Street Hotel have room services?

Yes, room service is available from 12 PM to 9 PM for your in-room dining needs.

Does Kloof Street Hotel provide daily housekeeping services?

Rooms are cleaned unobtrusively daily while you enjoy your meal. At no other time will you be disturbed unless you request assistance. Our staff is always available to assist wherever necessary.

Can I exchange currency at Kloof Street Hotel?

No, we do not offer currency exchange services at Kloof Street Hotel but our friendly front desk team will be able to point you in the right direction.

Is there Wi-Fi available at Kloof Street Hotel?

Yes, we offer strong and complimentary Wi-Fi throughout the hotel for all of your connectivity needs.

What amenities are included in the rooms at Kloof Street Hotel?

All rooms come equipped with a TV, minibar, coffee & tea making facilities, air conditioning, and free Wi-Fi.

Are pets allowed at Kloof Street Hotel?

While we love our furry friends, pets are not allowed at Kloof Street Hotel.

Is there a Conference Room, Function or Event space available at Kloof Street Hotel?

Yes, The Bailey Gallery & Conference Room on our ground floor provides a perfect venue for meetings, conferences, functions or events. Our restaurant also plays host to many special occasions, and packages or events can be customised depending on your needs. Contact our conference coordinator at cptconferences@lionroars.com or visit our website for package options.

Are there any job vacancies at Kloof Street Hotel?

You can view our job openings here . If there aren't any matching positions, visit our Lion Roars careers page for opportunities across our properties or drop your CV to reservations@lionroars.com.

How small is the Economy Pod Room at Kloof Street Hotel?

The entry level room type, our economy pod rooms are perfect for budget-conscious travellers. These rooms are compact, measuring 15 square meters yet still providing all the essentials for a comfortable stay, including a working desk, high-speed internet, and a King sized bed.

Can I work remotely from Kloof Street Hotel?

Absolutely - all our rooms are equipped with a working desk and high-speed internet, ideal for remote work. Additionally, our communal lounge area provides a perfect co-working space with various seating options, free Wi-Fi and delicious food and beverages to order. There is also a private boardroom / meeting space that can be hired should it be required.

Can I use the pool at Kloof Street Hotel if I’m NOT an in-house guest?

Yes, you are welcome to use the pool if you are not staying at the hotel, provided advance notice or a booking is made ahead of time. Kindly note that additional day rates for pool access do apply to external guests. Day rates are R200 per adult and R100 per child. To make a booking you can contact our friendly team at sanctuary@lionroars.com.

If you have any other questions or need more information, please don’t hesitate to contact our friendly team at reservations@lionroars.com or via WhatsApp (+27 (0)21 137 6581).
